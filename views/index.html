How To Play:
  • Click "Roll Dice" to start the first round.
  • Click "Roll Dice" again to play the second round.
  • The game ends after three rounds.
  • Click "New Game" to play again.
Rules:
  • You will be playing against the computer.
  • Each round, both players roll their dice.
  • The score of each player is tallied after a round.
  • After three rounds, the player with the highest total score wins.
  • If a player rolls a 1, their score for that round is 0.
  • If a player rolls a pair, their score for that round is double the sum of the pair (e.g. 3 & 3 is 2(3 + 3) = 12)
  • Otherwise, the player's score for that round is the sum of their rolls.
